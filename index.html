About Loro Coffee

Loro Coffee is a product of Loro Coffee Farms, specializing in growing Robusta organic coffee. Located in Loro Town Council, with farms in Loro Village and Wii Dam Apiny, Aber Sub County, established in 2018.

Our farms are established sustainably, protecting natural vegetation and environment around our farms.

Our Mission

To produce exceptional organic coffee while fostering economic development and social progress in rural communities.

Our Vision

To transform Loro into a renowned coffee hub, ensuring community benefits and regional growth.

Environmental Stewardship

We implement sustainable farming techniques and protect natural vegetation around our farms.

Community Empowerment

We support local farmers with training and resources, promoting ethical farming practices.

Economic Development

Our initiatives create jobs, improve infrastructure, and uplift rural communities.

Steps of manufacturing our products

01

Harvest occurs annually when the coffee beans reach maturity and are collected for processing.

02

The beans are dried using a wet or dry technique, depending on the taste we want to obtain.

03

The coffee is roasted and acquires its flavor by processing the grain in ovens.

Our Manufacturing Process

From carefully selected farms to your cup - precision at every stage.

A

Sourcing & Selection

We source premium beans from high-altitude farms known for exceptional soil and climate conditions. Every batch is selected based on traceability, sustainability and cup quality.

B

Harvesting

Only perfectly ripe cherries are hand-picked to ensure balanced sweetness and optimal sugar development for refined flavor profiles.

C

Quality Control

Each batch ungergoes professional cupping sessions to assess aroma, acidity, body, and aftertaste before approval.

D

Packaging

Freshness is sealed using one-way degassing valves and airtight packaging to preserve aroma and flavor intergrity.
